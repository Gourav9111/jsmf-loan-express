Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum loan amount I can get?

You can get a home loan up to ₹5 Crore, subject to your income, credit profile, and property value. The loan amount is typically 80-90% of the property value.

How long does the approval process take?

Our home loan approval process typically takes 7-10 working days from the submission of complete documents. In some cases, it may be faster depending on your profile.

Can I prepay my home loan?

Yes, you can prepay your home loan partially or fully. Most banks allow free prepayment for floating rate loans after a certain period, usually 6-12 months.

What are the tax benefits on home loans?

You can claim tax deduction on principal repayment up to ₹1.5 Lakhs under Section 80C and interest payment up to ₹2 Lakhs under Section 24 of the Income Tax Act.
