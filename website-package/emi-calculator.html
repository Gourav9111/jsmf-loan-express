EMI Planning Tips

Choose Right Tenure

Longer tenure means lower EMI but higher total interest. Balance EMI affordability with total cost.

Compare Interest Rates

Even a 0.5% difference in interest rate can save lakhs over the loan tenure. Compare offers from multiple banks.

Prepayment Benefits

Make prepayments when possible to reduce principal amount and save on interest payments.

EMI-to-Income Ratio

Keep your total EMIs (all loans) below 40% of your monthly income for comfortable repayment.
